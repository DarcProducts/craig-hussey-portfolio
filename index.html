Craig Hussey

Game Developer + Game Programmer
Hello everyone! I'm Craig Isaac Hussey, a game developer and programmer from the vibrant state of New Jersey.

I'm deeply immersed in the world of gaming, where my true passion lies. My craft revolves around creating extraordinary and captivating experiences that transport players to fantastical realms. Equipped with my programming skills and an unending well of enthusiasm, I'm driven by a mission to defy the laws of reality (and maybe even physics) through the games I create.

I have a knack for injecting humor into my work, whether it's through clever dialogue or delightful hidden surprises that bring a smile to your face. I firmly believe that fun should be an integral part of every line of code I write.

When I'm not busy saving virtual worlds from peril, you'll often find me embarking on real-life adventures that would even captivate Indiana Jones. I love to explore new places and hike. I also enjoy jumping waves on my waverunner, working on my vehicles, and relaxing in the sun.

If you're searching for a game programmer who can bring your dreams to life while simultaneously infusing joy and crafting unforgettable gaming experiences, then I'm the person for the job. Together, we'll embark on an epic quest to leave players awestruck and filled with laughter. My goal is to create gaming moments that leave a lasting impression on anyone who experiences them, regardless of their familiarity with gaming.

Feel free to drop me a line at 908-878-6399, or at craig_hussey@bloomfield.edu, or even at darcproducts@gmail.com.

I have worked as a game programmer with a focus on Unity, and I am deeply passionate about creating immersive experiences. From designing engaging gameplay mechanics to optimizing performance, I am dedicated to pushing the boundaries of what's possible in gaming. My experience includes courses in interactive multimedia audio production, data structures, digital game design, advanced game design and development, and more. I am eager to bring my skills, enthusiasm, and commitment to delivering memorable gaming experiences to your next project.

While my professional journey may be an untold tale, my passion for all things gaming knows no bounds. I stand as a fresh-faced hero, yearning to join the ranks of a seasoned team. Picture me as the trusty underdog sidekick, embracing the art of learning, infusing the table with offbeat creativity, and conjuring gaming sorcery that leaves players in awe, uttering, 'Whoa!' and questioning, 'How on earth did they pull that off?!' Together, let's forge an alliance, turning dreams into pixels!
